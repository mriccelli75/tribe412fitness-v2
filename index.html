Why Tribe 412 Fitness

At Tribe | 412 Fitness, we do things differently. We want you to get the most out of your workouts, and know that you have a community supporting you as you work towards your wellness goals.

Located in the heart of Fox Chapel, PA our classes are specifically designed to incorporate strength and conditioning, cardiovascular endurance and flexibility, so you get a full body workout every time.

Our Workouts

We encourage you to take different classes throughout the week to ensure that you keep your workouts fresh and cross-train your body.

INDOOR CYCLING

Pedal through hill climbs, sprints and many other challenging drills and exercises all while jamming to the beat.

HIIT

"All-Out" Effort Intervals that combine full-body strength training with high intensity cardio bursts designed to tone your body and improve your endurance

(sp)Intervals
